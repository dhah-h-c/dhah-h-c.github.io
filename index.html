The name bibliothèque de Ket is inspired by the word "bibliothèque", my favourite word in French. It is an ode to my partner and his family, who are French, and who continually inspire me to grow and create.

Reading vs listening.

Is that a question? Both reading physical books and listening to audiobooks offer unique experiences that will benefit you in different ways.

Reading

  • Improves focus, reading comprehension, and vocabulary retention.
  • Requires full attention.
  • Easier to pause, reflect, and re-read for a deeper understanding.
  • Self-paced, which reduces distractions and increases concentration.

Listening

  • Improves listening comprehension and exposure to new ideas.
  • Great for multitasking.
  • Makes books more accessible for people with visual impairments or reading difficulties.
  • Provides additional context through tone, inflection, and emotion, increasing engagement.
